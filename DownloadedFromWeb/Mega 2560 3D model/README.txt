Mega 2560 3D model by Mr_Spiffy on Thingiverse: https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:5239089

Summary:
This is a 3D model of an Arduino clone of the Mega 2560 microcontroller board.  I needed one for an enclosure design, but couldn't find one that had precise dimensions for the mounting holes.  Despite no officially-diagnosed OCD, my brain felt it necessary to lay out the full details of each component on the board.The board design was taken from a Rexqualis R3 version of the Mega 2560 board.  Components were measured for size and placement by hand, using calibrated calipers.  However, due to their hand-measured nature, they may be slightly out of position by a tiny bit.  I also have an Elegoo R3 version of the Mega 2560, and the layouts appear nearly-identical to each other.  I imagine there are other-branded boards which also have very similar layouts.  Note:  These boards do differ a bit from the official Arduino models.I've provided various versions of 3D models to put to all of your various uses.  STL, 3MF, STEP, and a fully-detailed and parametric version of the Fusion 360 file are available to download.  The Fusion 360 file is relatively-organized, with folders of solid bodies for each component on the board, and grouped items on the timeline to try and keep it more compact.  Hover over timeline groups to see their names and find items you wish to edit.
